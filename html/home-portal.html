FAQ

What is the QR-based system?
Our QR-based system is a modern approach to managing church attendance and events. Members receive unique QR codes that can be scanned for quick and efficient check-ins at church events and services.
How do I get my QR code?
After registering on the portal, you'll receive your personal QR code that can be downloaded or accessed through your account. You can either print it or show it on your mobile device.
Can the system handle recurring events and multiple schedules?
Yes, the system is designed to manage recurring events and multiple schedules efficiently. You can set up events that repeat daily, weekly, or monthly, and easily manage different schedules for various church activities.
How do I sign up for an account?
Click on the "Get Started" button and fill out the registration form with your personal information. Once submitted, your account will be created and you can start using our services.
Can I track my event attendance history?
Yes! In your dashboard, you can view your complete attendance history, including past events and upcoming registered events. This helps you stay organized and manage your church activities.
How do I receive notifications about new events?
Once logged in, you'll automatically receive notifications about new events. You can manage your notification preferences in your account settings to customize what updates you receive.
